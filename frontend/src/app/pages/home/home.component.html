Welcome dear polymath

"Lumen & Leaf", is a library management application that offers an intuitive solution for organizing and sharing your paper books. You are here for a reason. Come and find your way with your personal collection.

Découvrez avec nous

At the back of a small local library, an old book had lain dormant for years, covered in dust. One stormy evening, a little girl entered, soaking wet, seeking shelter. She stumbled across the forgotten book and, on opening it, discovered worlds she'd never imagined. As she turned the pages, the storm outside faded, replaced by the light of stories that warmed her heart. That evening, she realized that, even alone, you're never truly alone with a book in your hands.

Une infinité de possibiltés

Sitting in front of her window, Anastasia watched the rain trace paths on the glass. One day, without really knowing why, she picked up an old notebook and a pencil. At first, she scribbled timid words, clumsy sentences. Then, little by little, stories were born under her hand: worlds, imaginary friends, secret dreams. With every page she turned, Léa felt stronger, freer. Writing became her refuge and her happiness, a space where she could invent anything. A discreet smile lit up her face: she had just found her voice.
